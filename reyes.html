Reyes Trujillo

I met Reyes in 2015 through our friend Mo and the impression that I got from him was that he was a little bit stand offish. The more I got to know him, learning his interests and seeing that some of them align with my own. He's somebody that always has time for a friend wether it be listening to one of us talk about our life or just playing games and cracking jokes with us. On top of all that, he has chosen to dedicate his life to other people becoming a respiratory therapist. Truly a one of a kind friend.

Interests

Video Games

Reyes just like the rest of us love video games but he has a somewhat distinct taste in certain games. He loves league of legends and castlevania which are two franchises that I never particularyl got the hype about. That's the thing though, Reyes finds the best in those games despite their flaws.

Music

He loves music because who doesn't and his favorite artist is Gorillaz. His musical taste is still developing so it's really fun to just send him albums that I think he'd like. Me and my friends all enjoy music and Reyes is no exception.

Craft Beer

He introduced me to the wonders of craft beer. I didn't know anything about the subtle tates in each beer until Rey explained it. He helped me see the best qualities of this hobby and I look forward to the next time I can have a brew with my bud.

Rey and his wife Madeline
