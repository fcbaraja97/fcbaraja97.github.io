Dora Briceno

My beautiful grandmother. She has a heart as warm as a box of fresh donuts and she's much sweeter than that. Me and my grandma are like batman and robin. We would always go to the grocery store together and we would just sit around watching novellas or Judge Judy together. Whenever I need advice or just a someone to talk to she is always a phone call away. I could never express how much she means to me but I hope that I can show her while she's still here.

Interests

Novellas

One of the best memories I have of my grandmother is when I had the flu and she came over to take care of me. She made me pozole and we both sat in my living room and watched spanish novellas all day long. Each new twist and turn had us at the edge of our seat and we would laugh and cry together while we watched them. My grandma is many things and being my friend is one of them.

Cooking

I've been trying to lose weight recently but each time I go to visit my grandmother she makes me more food than I could eat. She's always made the tastiest burritos and tamales but my grandma's beans are the best ever. I guess you could say, I know she cooks with her heart and that's why everything she cooks taste so amazing.

Birds

My grandma always mentioned how much she loved birds. The freedom of flight really spoke to her and I think she can relate to them in a way. She loves blue jays the most and they are her favorite shade of blue.
