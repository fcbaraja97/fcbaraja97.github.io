Zohab Fareed

I met Zohab or Mo back in 2015 when I was play xbox. He was very funny, always making our online group of friends laugh until we cried. He and I could talk for what seemed like hours but it never occured to me how long we could talk for. He became one of my best friends through the years and I got the chance to meet him this past year for our friend Reyes' wedding.

Interests

Skating

From the minute I met Mo he always mentioned that he had either came home from skating or was going to be headed to the skatepark soon. His passion for the sport is very inspiring to me, it was so infectious you couldn't help but acknowledge it. He gave me one of his old boards as a symbol of our friendship and I have it kept away just so the temptation to skate doesn't infect me like it has Mo.

Video Games

Like I said, we met through video games so it would come as no surprise to anyone that Mo loves video games just as much as I do. His favorite game is either Dark souls or Devil may cry 4 but there's no doubt that Mo makes any game fun. He has a knack for finding funny glitches or mechanics in-game that I nor the rest of our group can even fathom to find. Mo is pretty terrible at fighting games but that has never stopped him from indulging in them.

Food

We all love food right? However, noone LOVES food more than Mo. He is always finding really tasty places to get food from and he has some very descriptive opinions about the flavors in the food. That being said he loves frozen chicken patties to a very detestable level. They're not my thing but Mo loves them and I love Mo so, they get a pass I guess.
